Our Story

Aanchal and Neel are high school sweethearts. They went to Clarksburg High School and met each other in 2009. They ended up having Spanish class together, and their teacher sat them, "randomly" next to each other. Since then, they bonded over culture, music, dance, and so much more. As they learned more about each other, they realized that there was something special. They ended up going to the same college where they shared a lot of the same activities and passions. Through everything, they knew that there was something special. On April 6th 2019, Neel finally surprised her by proposing with a flashmob dance.

Pre-Wedding Events

April 29th 2021

MEHNDI TBD

Mehndi, also known as Henna, is a natural paste applied to the body to create art, like a temporary tattoo. At the Mehndi function, Aanchal will be getting her hands, feet, arms, and legs done. Come join her and get your mehndi designs as well. Join us for food, music, and so many more fun festivities. Fun fact, it is tradition for both the bride and groom to hide each other's names in their mehndi.

April 30th 2021

GARBA TBD

Garba is religion dance performed primarily during Navratri as a form of prayer. It is also done in celebrationa as the families are preparing the bride and groom for marraige. Come learn a new dance style, eat great traditional food, and celebrate the couple. See this video (video to come) to get an idea of what to expect

The Wedding

May 1st 2021

CEREMONY 12:30PM - 4:00PM

The traditional indian ceremony will begin with the Baraat (procession of the groom). Neel and his family/friends will be coming as a large dancing crowd, while Aanchal's family will wait to welcome them and bring Neel to the Mundap (altar). After the baraat, we will await Aanchal's entry to the Mundap and continue the wedding ceremony. Following the ceremony, we will be taking pictures with all our guests. Note that we will have the light refreshments, tea, coffee, and water at the ceremony.

May 1st 2021

RECEPTION 5:45PM - 1:00AM

By now we are married and we just want celebrate and party with you all. Come enjoy a cocktail hour until 7:00PM. Following the cocktail will be our buffet dinner, speeches, dance performances, and open dance floor. We will be having an open bar, and just want to see everyone crowding the dance floor with us, all night. We can't wait to see you all there.
